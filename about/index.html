About

April 2, 2023 · 1 min · Amirul Abdul Aziz

Currently, I’m working as a Data Geoscientist at Kangean Energy Indonesia. My role revolves around well and seismic data quality control and maintenance. I’ve also worked at Reckitt, Ula, Kargo Technologies, and Pure Earth Indonesia.

I graduated from Universitas Brawijaya with a BE majoring in Geophysical Engineering. My undergraduate thesis topic was petrophysical modeling titled ‘Petrophysical Modeling and Analysis to Discover the Reservoir Potential and Drilling Zone Prediction of Kittiwake Field, North Sea, United Kingdom’. This study focuses on subsurface morphological interpretation and petrophysical analysis of the Kittiwake Field.

If you’d like to discuss more on my project or interested in working together, you can contact me via my email.
